First aid guidelines

  • Accident

    • Check if the person is breathing and has a pulse.

    call for an ambulance or rush the person to a hospital.

    Avoid feeding the victim.

    Treat the victim's wounds.

  • Heart Attack

    • Call 112 instantly in case you suspect someone is having a heart attack.

    Keep your local emergency contact on speed dial.

    Do not feed any medicine to the patient in an attempt to treat them.

    Begin CPR if the patient is losing consciousness.

  • Breathing Difficulties

    • The person to safety and into fresh air if it is safe to do so otherwise,filter the smoke with a p2 mask.

    if the person is conscious,sit them down or lay them on their side.

    if the person is alert, ask them whether they have any medical conditions.

    if the person is not breathing, perform CPR.

  • fracture

    • Encourage the victim do not move the injured part.

    Check the injured part for swelling, bleeding and wound.

    Always raise the fractured part to control bleeding.

    Apply pressure on the bleeding site with sterile cloth.

  • cuts and wounds

    • Change the dressing at least daily or whenever the bandage becomes wet or dirty.

    Wash your hands-help prevent infection.

    Apply a thin layer of antibotic oinment or petroleum jelly.

    cover the wound with a bandaid or clean gauze&paer tape.
